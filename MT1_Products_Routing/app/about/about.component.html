ABOUT UNIQLO

UNIQLO is a clothing apparel company, which was originally founded in Yamaguchi,
Japan in 1949 as a textiles manufacturer. Now it is a global brand with over 1000
stores around the world. Redefining clothing, with a focus on quality and textiles
which has been unwavered since the company’s origins in 1949.

Uniqlo is a Japanese casual wear clothing store and brand that has gained international
